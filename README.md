# Tuckshop POS Beta

**Free Beta Version** for spaza and tuckshop owners in South Africa.

A simple, fast, and mobile-friendly Point of Sale system built for offline-first use with Google Sheets sync.

### Key Features

- Barcode scanning with phone camera (html5-qrcode)
- Manual search with quantity modal (Add or Multiples)
- Automatic selling price calculation with category markup + per-item override
- Accurate cash handling and change calculation (fixed cents precision)
- Stock management with expiry dates, min stock levels, and stock inspector
- Manage up to 15 categories with individual default markups
- Daily sales counter and reset
- Last 5 transactions log
- Float management and Cash Up reconciliation
- Offline-first with local storage
- Installable as PWA (add to home screen)

### Recent Updates

- Scanner stays open until manually closed with "Close Camera"
- Quantity modal now appears when selecting from search results
- Faster sync — only sold items are updated after each sale
- Improved cash tendering (exact amounts and cents now work reliably)
- Pending sync button appears automatically when needed

### How to Set Up

1. Open the app on your phone:  
   `https://yourusername.github.io/tuckshop-pos-beta/`

2. Go to the **Settings** tab

3. Tap **"Create New Shared Sheet"** (or paste your own Google Sheet URL)

4. Open the created sheet and set sharing to **"Anyone with the link" → Editor**

5. Copy the full sheet URL and paste it back into the app

6. Tap **"Save My Sheet & Sync"**

7. Start selling! Scan items or use search → choose quantity → complete sale

### Tips for Best Use

- Scan in good lighting for best results
- Camera remains open after scanning — use "Close Camera" when finished
- Stock updates automatically after sales (or use manual sync button)
- Works offline — all data is saved locally
