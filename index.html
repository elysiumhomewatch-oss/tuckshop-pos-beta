📖 How to Use Tuckshop POS Free ▼
Welcome!

1. Settings Tab → Paste your Google Sheet URL and tap "Save My Sheet & Sync"
2. Tap "Refresh Inventory from My Sheet" to load your products
3. Sale Tab: Scan barcodes or search → items go into cart
4. When ready, tap "Complete Sale" → enter cash received → see change to give back
5. Stock Tab: Scan or type barcode → fill name & cost → Save
6. Change markups anytime in Settings

Everything saves automatically on your phone. No internet needed after initial setup.
