📖 How to Use Tuckshop POS Beta ▼
1. Tap "Create New Shared Sheet" (recommended for new users)
2. Paste the copied URL and tap "Save My Sheet & Sync"
3. Tap "Refresh Inventory from My Sheet" to load your products
4. Go to Sale tab: Scan items or search → add to cart
5. Tap "Complete Sale" → enter cash received → see exact change
6. Use Stock tab to add or edit items
7. Change markups anytime in Settings
